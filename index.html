Mankato Tech Services is here to get your technology working so that you can get back to what is important to you.

Who We Serve

We serve both business and residential customers in the greater Mankato area. Whether you're a small business in need of on-site services, a college student with a failing hard drive that contains your midterm paper, or a parent of a teenager whose device may have just taken a swim in the toilet, we have you covered.

No Pressure To Buy

We don't think you should have to pay just to find out what is broken or what your options are. That is why we offer free diagnostics as well as free on-site consultations. We take pride in walking you through everything before asking you to make any decisions. We will always be honest and truthful with you, and we will never try to sell you services you simply don't need or pressure you to buy services that you don't want.
