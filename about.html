Meet Your Technician

Ben has over 15 years of experience in the technology industry. He holds degrees in Network Administration, Microcomputer Support Technician, and Office Systems Specialist. Ben also holds certifications for CompTIA Network+, CompTIA A+ and Microsoft Office Specialist Master.

Ben has a keen attention to detail and a passion for getting things done right. He takes pride in his work and focuses on surpassing customer expectations.

Ben is a native Minnesotan, having grown up in a small town just 30 miles from Mankato. He is proud to call Mankato home and enjoys working with people and businesses in the local community.

In his free time, Ben enjoys spending time in the great outdoors, including working in the garden, bicycling, hiking, kayaking, whitewater rafting, and he is always down for playing a good game of pickleball!
